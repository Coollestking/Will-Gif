Mr. Akongbabre Williams Ayaaba After practicing and teaching Arts for so many years, He has come to realise the very bottlenecks that impede the teaching and learning of Creative Arts at various levels in schools an fields where Arts is studied, hence the birth of this book in titled; 'THE RESCUE TO THE TEACHING AND LEARING OF CREATIVE ARTS ' This book covers all the National teaching syllabi of the Ghana Educati on Service (GES) on Creative Arts for primary six

Mr Williams

Williams Ayaaba Akongbabre is a talented Arist who, since age 13, has practised Creative Arts. He has over the years, demonstrated mastery over the subject due to his natural talent and 'finesse' that flow so easily from his teaching of Arts at the Senoir High and Primary school levels, as a part-timer since 2006. The passionate Artist is a doer and lover of Arts and a Motivational Speaker who has impackted many lives and produced numerous Artworks especially in the Upper Regions of Ghana as well as some parts of the neighbouring Togo and Burkina Foso
